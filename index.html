About Me

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Jeannette Kunzen. My friends would describe me as loyal, honest and artistic. Back in 2005 I graduated from the Savannah College of Art & Design (SCAD) with a BFA in Graphic Design which was followed by an 11 year career at Harcourt School Publishers.

After this my technical design knowledge has been applied to leasing and sales support materials at a Commercial Real Estate company, going on three years. To balance my tendency toward company loyalty I also like to keep busy with new and varied experiences. Some examples of side jobs I have taken include working at a convenience store and painting murals.

Orlando has been home for the past 15 years with family roots that go back 3 generations, and I spent the first 18 years of my life in South Florida while my father practiced architecture there. Other places I've lived include Savannah, Georgia and Durham, North Carolina. Despite being a native Floridian I've been snow skiing since the age of 10 during family trips to Utah and Colorado.

My hobbies include fitness, crafts, and motorcyles. My ideal vacation would be spent traveling overseas. Some countries I've visited on my own include Austria and India. My husband and I are becoming seasoned travelers to Japan and try to get over there at least once a year.
